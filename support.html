Data Deletion Instructions

You have full control over your data at all times:

  • Disconnect social accounts: You can disconnect your TikTok, YouTube, or Instagram account at any time. This immediately revokes our publishing access and we delete the associated tokens within 24 hours.
  • Delete your account: Email us at business.ai.gpt@gmail.com to request full account deletion. We will delete all your data within 30 days and send confirmation.
  • Revoke access from the platform side: You can also revoke AIKnow-IT's access directly from your TikTok, YouTube, or Instagram account settings.

FAQ

Does AIKnow-IT store my social media passwords?

No. We use OAuth 2.0 for all social media connections. Your passwords are never shared with or stored by AIKnow-IT.

Can I review content before it's published?

Yes. You can configure your account to require manual approval before any video is published to your social media accounts.

What happens if a video fails to publish?

You'll receive an email notification with details about which platform failed and why. The video remains available so you can retry or post manually.

Is my data shared with third parties?

We only share data with the social media platforms you've connected (for publishing) and OpenAI (for content generation, with no personal data included). See our Privacy Policy for full details.
